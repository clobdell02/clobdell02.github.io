About Me:

Hi, my name is Colton and creatively solving problems with the use of technology is a passion of mine. My interest for creativley working with technology started at a young age with me trying to understand how the video games I was playing worked. This eventually led me to pursuing academic teaching in fields such as web design in 2011, and robotics in 2012. I continued wanting to and trying to learn about technology until eventually majoring in Computer and Information Science at the University of Oregon.

Now with just having graduated in June of 2022 with my Bachelors, I have continued to try and learn more while creating a career around software development. My career goal is to enter and succeed within the game development or mobile application design fields. Currently I am focused on finding a place where I can help produce software that will benefit people while being creative, reusable, and intuitive.
